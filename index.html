A website to plot various number sequences on different grids and observe patterns.

The grids are square shaped and we number the grid elements:

Grid type Numbers
Rows 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9
Rows alternate 1 2 3 6 5 4 7 8 9
Spiral 5 4 3 6 1 2 7 8 9

Then for a given sequence, we place dots at the positions on the grid corresponding to elements in the sequence.
